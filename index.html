Im Glad You Asked...

Hey! Do you know how many meals you had last week or even what you ate last night? How did it taste? Did you enjoy how the last forkful of your meat dish with rice tasted like babies smiling when they see their mother??

Well for that exact reason, Karen Yearwood, Mayankho Trumble, and Joshua Stevens came up with the answer to the simplest question everyone hasnt asked themselves yet...What did I eat and How can I keep a record of this?

We introduce "What's In Your Belly?" The website application that records your dines and reminds you of what you ate by categorizing all of your prior meals.
